<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) decking market is primarily divided into capped and uncapped composite types. Capped composite decking features a protective layer on the surface, enhancing durability, resistance to stains, and fading, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. In contrast, uncapped composite decking lacks this protective layer, which may make it more susceptible to weathering and wear but typically offers a more cost-effective solution. Both types cater to different customer preferences regarding aesthetics, performance, and budgets.</p></p>
