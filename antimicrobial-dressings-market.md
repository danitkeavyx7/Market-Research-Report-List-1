<p><p>The antimicrobial dressings market features various types designed to promote healing and prevent infection. Silver wound dressings utilize silver’s antibacterial properties, effectively reducing microbial growth. Iodine antimicrobial dressings release iodine to combat bacteria and manage exudate. Honey antimicrobial dressings leverage honey’s natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory qualities, enhancing wound healing. Polyhexamethylene biguanide (PHMB) dressings provide broad-spectrum antimicrobial action and are used for both acute and chronic wounds, making them versatile in clinical applications. Each type offers unique benefits tailored for specific wound management needs.</p></p>
