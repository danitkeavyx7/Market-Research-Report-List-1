<p><p>The noninvasive cancer biomarkers market includes various types that aid in cancer detection and monitoring. Protein-based biomarkers focus on specific proteins associated with tumors, while DNA-based biomarkers analyze genetic mutations linked to cancer. mRNA-based biomarkers examine gene expression patterns that may indicate malignancy, and microRNA-based biomarkers detect small RNA molecules that regulate gene expression in cancerous cells. These categories are crucial for early diagnosis, prognosis, and personalized treatment strategies, enhancing patient management and outcomes.</p></p>
