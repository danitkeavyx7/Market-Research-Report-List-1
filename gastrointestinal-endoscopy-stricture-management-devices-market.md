<p><p>Gastrointestinal endoscopy stricture management devices are essential in treating narrowing of the gastrointestinal tract. Balloon dilators are inflatable devices inserted to widen strictures, restoring passage. Stents are tube-like structures temporarily placed to keep the narrowed area open, providing sustained relief. Bougie dilators are tapered instruments used to gradually widen strictures through a series of progressive sizes. Each device plays a critical role in improving patient outcomes, enhancing the efficiency of endoscopic procedures, and alleviating symptoms related to strictures.</p></p>
