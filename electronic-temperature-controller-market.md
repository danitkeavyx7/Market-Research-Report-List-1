<p><strong>The Electronic Temperature Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Thermocouple-type"</li><li>"Resistor-type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Temperature Controller market comprises two main types: thermocouple-type and resistor-type controllers. </p><p>Thermocouple-type controllers utilize thermocouples to measure temperature through the voltage generated at the junction of two different metals, providing rapid response and high-temperature range capabilities. In contrast, resistor-type controllers, like RTDs (Resistance Temperature Detectors), measure temperature by correlating resistance changes in metals with temperature variations, offering high accuracy and stability at lower temperatures. Both types are crucial in industrial and commercial applications, ensuring precise temperature regulation.</p></p>
