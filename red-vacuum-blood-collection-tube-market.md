Common Serum Tubes are designed to collect blood for routine laboratory tests, allowing for serum separation after clotting. Rapid Serum Tubes, on the other hand, enable faster serum collection by incorporating additives that accelerate the clotting process. Both types are essential for various diagnostic applications, catering to the needs of healthcare providers in managing patient testing efficiently.</p></p>
