<p><p>The construction films market includes various polymer types, each serving specific applications. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) is known for its flexibility and strength, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers good moisture resistance. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is valued for its durability. PP/BOPP (Polypropylene/Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) provides excellent barrier properties. PET/BOPET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is used for its clarity and durability. Polyamide (BOPA) films are strong and versatile, PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) offers adhesion, and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is known for its rigidity and water resistance.</p></p>
