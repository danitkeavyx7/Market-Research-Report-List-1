<p><strong>The Cardiac Catheterization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angiography Catheters</li><li>Guiding Catheters</li><li>Pulmonary Artery Catheters</li><li>PTCA Balloon Catheters</li><li>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Catheters</li><li>Electrophysiology Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac catheterization market consists of various types of catheters used in diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases. Angiography catheters facilitate imaging of blood vessels, while guiding catheters assist in accessing specific areas in the heart. Pulmonary artery catheters enable hemodynamic monitoring, and PTCA balloon catheters are essential for angioplasty procedures. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheters provide detailed imaging of arterial walls, and electrophysiology catheters are utilized for studying and treating arrhythmias. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing cardiac care.</p></p>
