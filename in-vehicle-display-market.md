<p><p>The in-vehicle display market features several types, including TFT LCD, PMOLED, PMLCD, and AMOLED displays. TFT LCDs are widely used for their affordability and brightness. PMOLEDs, with their simpler structures, are suitable for low-resolution applications. PMLCDs combine the benefits of TFT and PMOLED, offering better contrast and color. AMOLED displays deliver superior color accuracy and flexibility, making them ideal for high-end applications. The "Others" category may include emerging technologies like Mini LED and microLED displays, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
