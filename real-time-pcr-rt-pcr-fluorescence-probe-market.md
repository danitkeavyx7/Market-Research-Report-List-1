<p><p>The real-time PCR fluorescence probe market includes several key types: TaqMan probes, which utilize a specific reporter and quencher for precise quantification; Molecular Beacons, which form a stem-loop structure that fluoresces upon hybridization with target DNA; and Minor Groove Binder Oligodeoxynucleotide Conjugates (MGB-ODNs), which enhance specificity and enable detection of shorter sequences. Each type offers unique advantages for real-time amplification and quantification, catering to diverse applications in research, diagnostics, and clinical testing.</p></p>
