<p><p>The Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) treatment market can be categorized based on the delivery method of sperm. Intrauterine involves placing sperm directly into the uterus, enhancing chances of fertilization. Intracervical involves sperm placement in the cervix, aiming to facilitate natural ascent toward the uterus. Intravaginal entails depositing sperm in the vagina, relying on natural sperm mobility. Intratubal transfers sperm directly to the fallopian tubes, targeting egg retrieval potential. Each method offers different pathways for achieving conception based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
