<p><strong>The LED Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Lit LED Monitor</li><li>Edge Lit LED monitor</li><li>Full Array LED Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED monitor market includes three main types: Direct Lit LED, Edge Lit LED, and Full Array LED monitors. Direct Lit LED monitors feature backlighting directly behind the screen, providing uniform brightness and better contrast. Edge Lit LED monitors place LEDs along the edges, allowing for thinner designs but may have uneven lighting. Full Array LED monitors also use a grid of LEDs behind the screen, enabling local dimming for improved picture quality and contrast across different content.</p></p>
