<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Power Generator</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Power Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power generator market primarily comprises two types: horizontal-axis wind power generators (HAWT) and vertical-axis wind power generators (VAWT). HAWTs are the most prevalent, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, ideal for high-efficiency energy production in areas with consistent wind. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, offering advantages in urban environments due to their compact design and less sensitivity to wind direction. Both types play crucial roles in diversifying renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
