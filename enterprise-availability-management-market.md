<p><p>Enterprise Availability Management encompasses two primary market types: Local Deployment and Cloud Deployment. Local Deployment involves installing software on on-premises servers, ensuring complete control over data and customizable configurations, which appeals to organizations with strict security needs. In contrast, Cloud Deployment delivers availability management through hosted services, providing scalability, cost-effectiveness, and simplified maintenance. Organizations often choose between these models based on their operational requirements, resources, and preferences for data control versus convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
