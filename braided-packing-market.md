<p><strong>The Braided Packing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Square Braided</li><li>Braid Over Braid</li><li>Braid Over Core</li><li>Interbraid</li><li>Die Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The braided packing market encompasses various types designed for different applications. Square braided packing features a square cross-section, offering efficient sealing and durability. Braid over braid packing consists of multiple braided layers for enhanced flexibility and strength. Braid over core packing includes a braided exterior over a solid core, providing structural support. Interbraid packing combines different braiding techniques for specialized requirements. Die form packing is pre-formed to specific shapes, ensuring precise fit and minimal leakage in demanding environments.</p></p>
