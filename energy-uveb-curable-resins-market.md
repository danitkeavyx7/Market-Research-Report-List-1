<p><strong>The Energy (UV/EB) Curable Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Curing</li><li>Electron beam (EB) Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Energy Curable Resins market encompasses two primary types: Ultraviolet (UV) Curing and Electron Beam (EB) Curing. UV curing uses ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, ideal for fast-drying coatings and inks, while EB curing employs high-energy electrons to achieve cross-linking, providing excellent surface hardness and durability. Both methods are eco-friendly due to reduced volatile organic compound emissions and are widely utilized in industries like automotive, electronics, and packaging, driving innovation in sustainable manufacturing practices.</p></p>
