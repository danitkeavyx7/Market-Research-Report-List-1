<p><strong>The Brake Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Brake Testers</li><li>Plate Brake Testers</li><li>Decelerometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake tester market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Roller Brake Testers**: These systems assess a vehicle's braking performance by rolling the wheels over calibrated rollers, measuring force and friction.</p><p>2. **Plate Brake Testers**: Utilizing a fixed plate, this type measures brake efficiency as the vehicle's wheels press against the surface, providing detailed performance data.</p><p>3. **Decelerometers**: Portable devices that measure a vehicle's deceleration rates during braking, helping evaluate overall brake effectiveness and safety. Each type serves to enhance vehicle safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
