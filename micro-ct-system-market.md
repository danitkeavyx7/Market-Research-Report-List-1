<p><p>The Micro CT system market can be categorized into In Vivo and Ex Vivo types. In Vivo micro CT involves imaging living organisms, allowing for the assessment of biological processes and structures in their natural state, often used in medical and pharmacological studies. Ex Vivo micro CT, on the other hand, involves imaging tissues or organs after they have been removed from the organism, providing detailed insights into anatomy and pathological changes. Both types are essential for advancing research and diagnostics.</p></p>
