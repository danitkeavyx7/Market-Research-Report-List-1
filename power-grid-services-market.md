<p><p>Power Grid Services markets focus on enhancing grid reliability and efficiency through various means. Reactive Power Compensation addresses voltage stability by managing reactive power flow. Voltage Regulation ensures consistent voltage levels across the grid. Flicker Control mitigates fluctuations in voltage that can impact sensitive equipment. Active Power Filtering removes unwanted disturbances, improving overall power quality. Harmonic Cancellation reduces harmonics generated by non-linear loads, preventing potential damage and inefficiencies. Together, these services support a stable and efficient power grid.</p></p>
