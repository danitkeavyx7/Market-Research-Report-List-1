<p><strong>The Cutting Electrosurgical Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Electrosurgery</li><li>Monopolar Electrosurgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting electrosurgical unit market is primarily divided into bipolar and monopolar electrosurgery. Bipolar electrosurgery involves both the active and return electrodes in the surgical instrument, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation with minimal thermal spread, making it suitable for delicate tissues. In contrast, monopolar electrosurgery uses a single active electrode and a dispersive return pad, facilitating greater versatility in various procedures, including larger tissue cutting. Each method has unique advantages based on the surgical requirements and tissue types involved.</p></p>
