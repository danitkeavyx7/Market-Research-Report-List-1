<p><p>Near Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) is categorized into various types based on the detection methods employed. Single beam NIRS analyzes the intensity of light from a single source, making it simpler and often less expensive but more susceptible to environmental variations. Double beam NIRS uses two beams—one directed at the sample and another for reference—improving sensitivity and accuracy by compensating for fluctuations in light source intensity. Other types may include advanced systems with combined techniques for enhanced performance in diverse applications.</p></p>
