<p><p>The Steel Mill Skimmer market is categorized mainly into two types: Belt Type and Disc Type. Belt Type skimmers utilize a continuous belt mechanism to collect and remove slag and impurities from the surface of molten metal. In contrast, Disc Type skimmers feature rotating discs that efficiently skim surface materials, offering precise control. Both types are essential in steel manufacturing for improving product quality and ensuring operational efficiency, adapting to various production needs and environments in steel mills.</p></p>
