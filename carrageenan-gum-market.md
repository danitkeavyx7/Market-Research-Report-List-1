<p><strong>The Carrageenan Gum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Iota</li><li>Kappa</li><li>Lambda</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrageenan gum market is segmented into three primary types: Iota, Kappa, and Lambda. Iota carrageenan forms gels in the presence of calcium ions, making it suitable for dairy and meat applications. Kappa carrageenan, which gels with potassium ions, is commonly used in dairy desserts and processed foods. Lambda carrageenan remains soluble and does not gel, making it ideal for sauces and dairy applications. Each type has distinct functional properties and various applications across the food and cosmetics industries.</p></p>
