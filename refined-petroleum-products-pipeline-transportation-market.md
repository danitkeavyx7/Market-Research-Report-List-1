<p><ul><li>Gathering Pipelines</li><li>Transportation Pipelines</li><li>Distribution Pipelines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refined petroleum products pipeline transportation market consists of three key types: gathering pipelines, transportation pipelines, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines collect crude oil or refined products from production sites and transport them to processing facilities. Transportation pipelines move larger volumes of refined products over long distances, connecting refineries to major distribution hubs. Distribution pipelines then deliver these products to local markets, including gas stations and industrial users, ensuring efficient delivery from large-scale transport to end-user access.</p></p>
