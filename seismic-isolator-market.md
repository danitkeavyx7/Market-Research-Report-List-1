<p><p>The seismic isolator market comprises various types designed to enhance structural resilience during earthquakes. Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB) utilize rubber's elasticity to absorb seismic energy, while Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB) feature a lead core for added damping and flexibility. High Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR) offer superior energy dissipation through advanced materials, optimizing performance in seismic events. Other types may include friction pendulum systems or sliding bearings, each providing unique advantages in protecting structures from seismic forces and improving safety and longevity.</p></p>
