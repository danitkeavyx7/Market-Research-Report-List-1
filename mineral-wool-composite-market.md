<p><p>The Mineral Wool Composite market is primarily categorized into Rock Wool Composite and Glass Wool Composite. Rock Wool Composite is made from natural basalt rock, offering excellent thermal insulation, fire resistance, and sound absorption, making it ideal for industrial applications. Glass Wool Composite, on the other hand, is produced from silica and other minerals, providing lightweight insulation with high resistance to moisture and chemical degradation. Both types serve diverse construction and insulation needs, enhancing energy efficiency and safety.</p></p>
