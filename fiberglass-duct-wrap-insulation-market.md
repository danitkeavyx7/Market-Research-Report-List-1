<p><p>Fiberglass duct wrap insulation is designed to enhance energy efficiency and minimize noise in HVAC systems. It is classified into two main types: insulation with facings and insulation without facings. Insulation with facings includes protective layers such as foil or paper, which enhance moisture resistance and enhance appearance, making it ideal for exposed applications. Insulation without facings lacks these layers, providing a cost-effective option for concealed or less demanding environments, focusing on thermal performance without additional protective features.</p></p>
