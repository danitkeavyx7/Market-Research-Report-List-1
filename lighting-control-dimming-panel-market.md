<p><p>The lighting control dimming panel market is divided into two primary types: rack-mount and wall-mount systems. Rack-mount panels are designed for installation in standard server racks, providing a centralized solution for controlling multiple lighting circuits and systems in commercial environments. In contrast, wall-mount dimming panels are directly affixed to walls, allowing for easier access and control in residential or smaller settings. Both types enhance energy efficiency and user convenience while allowing tailored lighting solutions for various applications.</p></p>
