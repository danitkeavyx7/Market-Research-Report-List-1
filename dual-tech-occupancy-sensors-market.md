<p><p>Dual tech occupancy sensors combine passive infrared (PIR) and ultrasonic technologies to enhance occupancy detection accuracy. Ceiling mount occupancy sensors are typically installed on ceilings, ideal for large, open areas, detecting movement from above. In contrast, wall mount occupancy sensors are placed on walls, providing effective coverage in smaller spaces or corridors. Both types help optimize energy use by automatically controlling lighting and HVAC systems based on occupancy, making them essential for modern energy-efficient building designs.</p></p>
