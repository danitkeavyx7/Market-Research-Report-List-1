<p><p>The Telecom Power Inductors market consists of SMD (Surface Mount Device) Power Inductors and Plug-in Power Inductors. SMD Power Inductors are designed for compact spaces, featuring a flat profile that allows for easy mounting on circuit boards, making them ideal for modern electronic devices. Plug-in Power Inductors, on the other hand, are used in applications requiring higher power handling and are typically installed through holes in circuit boards. Both types are essential for efficient power management in telecommunications equipment.</p></p>
