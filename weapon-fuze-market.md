<p><p>The weapon fuze market is categorized into two main types: mechanical and electronic. Mechanical fuzes operate through physical mechanisms, utilizing springs, gears, or other mechanical components to trigger detonation. They are often simpler and more reliable in harsh conditions. Electronic fuzes, on the other hand, utilize electrical components and sensors for enhanced precision and programmable capabilities, allowing for advanced functions like time delays and impact sensitivity. Both types cater to different military needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
