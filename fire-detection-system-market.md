<p><ul><li>Addressable Fire Detection Systems</li><li>Conventional Fire Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire detection system market is primarily categorized into addressable and conventional systems. Addressable fire detection systems use intelligent technology to identify the exact location of a fire by assigning individual addresses to each device, allowing for more precise monitoring and quicker response times. In contrast, conventional fire detection systems operate on a zone-based approach, which triggers alarms for entire areas without pinpointing the exact source of the fire, making them less efficient in large or complex structures.</p></p>
