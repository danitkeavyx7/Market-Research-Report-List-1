<p><p>Semiconductor defect inspection systems are critical for ensuring the quality and reliability of chips. The wafer inspection system focuses on detecting defects in the silicon wafers during the fabrication process, using advanced imaging techniques to identify anomalies that can impact performance. The mask inspection system, on the other hand, inspects the photomasks used in lithography, ensuring they are free from defects before transferring patterns onto wafers. Both systems play vital roles in maintaining manufacturing integrity and yield in semiconductor production.</p></p>
