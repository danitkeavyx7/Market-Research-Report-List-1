In contrast, capillary columns feature a narrow internal diameter, allowing for high-resolution analysis and faster analysis times. Both column types are essential in laboratories for various applications, including chemical analysis and research, serving industries such as pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, and environmental testing.</p></p>
