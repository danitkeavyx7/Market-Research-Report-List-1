<p><p>Sulfone polymers consist of various types, each with unique properties and applications. Polysulfone (PSU) boasts excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it ideal for medical devices and aerospace components. Polyethersulfone (PES) offers superior chemical resistance and is commonly used in filtration membranes and electrical insulation. Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU) exhibits high heat resistance and toughness, suitable for high-performance applications in automotive and healthcare industries. Collectively, these polymers serve diverse sectors, driven by their specific characteristics and performance attributes.</p></p>
