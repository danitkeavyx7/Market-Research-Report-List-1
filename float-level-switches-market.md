<p><p>Float level switches are crucial for monitoring liquid levels in various applications, and they come in three main types. Top-mounted type switches are installed at the tank's top, ideal for applications where high liquid levels need detection. Side-mounted type switches attach to the tank's side, suitable for narrow spaces and medium level detection. Bottom-mounted type switches sit at the tank's bottom, effectively detecting low liquid levels. Each type offers distinct advantages based on installation space, application, and measurement needs.</p></p>
