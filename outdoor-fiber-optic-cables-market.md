<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor fiber optic cables market comprises primarily two types: single-mode and multi-mode fiber optic cables. Single-mode cables allow light to travel in a single pathway, enabling long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication and wide-area networks. In contrast, multi-mode cables support multiple light paths, which facilitates shorter-distance communication and is generally used for local area networks and within buildings. Together, these cables cater to various connectivity needs in diverse environments.</p></p>
