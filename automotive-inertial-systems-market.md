<p><p>The Automotive Inertial Systems market is categorized into four main types: gyroscopes, accelerometers, inertial measurement units (IMUs), and other components. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity, aiding in vehicle stability and navigation. Accelerometers detect changes in velocity, contributing to safety systems and performance monitoring. IMUs combine gyroscopes and accelerometers for comprehensive motion tracking, enhancing autonomous driving and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Other market components may include sensors and software for data processing and integration, further advancing automotive technology.</p></p>
