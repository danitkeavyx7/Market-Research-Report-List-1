<p><p>The multipurpose kitchen scale market primarily features two types: mechanical scales and digital scales. Mechanical scales rely on a spring mechanism, providing a simple, battery-free option that is often more durable but less precise. In contrast, digital scales utilize electronic sensors to deliver accurate measurements and typically feature displays for easy reading. Digital models may offer additional functionalities, such as tare and unit conversion, appealing to consumers seeking precision and convenience in their cooking and baking tasks.</p></p>
