<p><ul><li>Optical Sensor</li><li>Capacitive Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive rain sensing wiper systems enhance driver safety by automatically adjusting wiper speed based on rain intensity. The market features primarily two types of sensors: optical sensors and capacitive sensors. Optical sensors utilize infrared light to detect raindrops on the windshield, enabling precise control of wiper operation. In contrast, capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance caused by water droplets on the glass surface, allowing for responsive wiper adjustments. Both technologies contribute to improved visibility and convenience during adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
