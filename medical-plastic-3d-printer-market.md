<p><p>The medical plastic 3D printer market comprises several types, including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Fused Granular Fabrication (FGF), and Laser Metal Fusion (LMF), among others. FDM uses thermoplastic filaments to create structures layer by layer, widely utilized for prototypes and models. FGF involves melting granules to build larger parts, offering cost efficiency for production. LMF focuses on metal applications for medical devices, allowing for intricate designs. Other technologies include stereolithography and digital light processing, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
