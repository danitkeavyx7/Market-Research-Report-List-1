<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Quick Lock Pin market is categorized into three main types: Single Acting, Double Acting, and Detent Pins. Single Acting pins engage in one direction, providing quick locking but requiring manual release. Double Acting pins offer two-way operation, allowing for both locking and unlocking actions with a pull or push mechanism. Detent Pins incorporate a spring-loaded ball that secures the pin in place, offering enhanced stability and ease of use in various aircraft applications.</p></p>
