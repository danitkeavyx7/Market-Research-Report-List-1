<p><p>The PPKTP waveguide market is segmented into two main types: monolithic PPKTP and half monolithic PPKTP. Monolithic PPKTP waveguides consist of a single, continuous crystal structure, ensuring high optical quality and performance with minimal loss. In contrast, half monolithic PPKTP combines a PPKTP crystal with a separate substrate, allowing for easier integration with other components while still maintaining decent performance. Both types cater to different application needs, ranging from telecommunications to quantum optics.</p></p>
